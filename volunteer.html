Volunteer

Help us make athleticism accessible for everyone.

Why Volunteer with Us?

Goalball wouldn't be possible without our dedicated volunteers. From ensuring the court is set up correctly to assisting athletes with navigation, our volunteers are the backbone of our community.

Whether you're a student looking for service hours, a sports enthusiast, or simply someone who wants to give back to the San Diego community, there's a place for you on our court. We practice regularly at the Stagecoach Community Center in Carlsbad and the Mission Valley YMCA in San Diego. No prior experience with goalball or visual impairments is necessary—we provide all the training you need.

Volunteer Roles

Goal Judge

Help determine if a ball crossed the goal line and assist in returning the ball to the players.

Timer/Scorer

Manage the game clock, track the 10-second rule, and maintain the official score sheet during matches.

Sighted Guide

Assist athletes with navigation around the gym facility and during community events.

Court Setup

Help lay down the tactile floor lines (string and tape) before practice starts.

Getting Started

Volunteer Requirements

  • Minimum age of 15 (or accompanied by an adult)
  • Punctual and reliable
  • Respectful and inclusive attitude
