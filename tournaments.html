Tournaments

The pinnacle of competitive goalball.

Competitive Excellence

Tournaments are the highlight of our season. They offer our athletes the chance to test their skills against teams from across the region and the country. Competition builds camaraderie, resilience, and a deeper understanding of the sport.

San Diego Goalball travels regularly to several major tournaments throughout the year. We compete in both regional qualifiers and invitationals.

Key Annual Events

Cascade Classic

Portland, OR | February

One of the longest-running and most prestigious tournaments on the West Coast. Hosted by Adaptive Sports NW, it features elite and developmental divisions.

Learn More

USABA Nationals

Varies | June/July

The ultimate domestic competition. The USABA National Championships bring together the best clubs from across the United States to crown a national champion.

How We Prepare

Intensive Training

Leading up to a tournament, we increase our practice frequency and focus on tactical drills, conditioning, and team communication.

Travel Logistics

Traveling as a team involves coordinating flights, accessible housing, and transportation of specialized equipment like our goalball court tape and balls.
