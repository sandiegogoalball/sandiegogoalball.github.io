Our Mission

Empowering Athletes Through Sound & Spirit

San Diego County Goalball provides a supportive yet competitive environment for athletes of all skill levels. Founded by Neal and Lori Meyers to support their son Michael, we are dedicated to the Paralympic sport of goalball.

Whether you're looking for recreation, fitness, or aiming for the highest levels of competition, our world-class coaches and welcoming community have a place for you on our court.

Video Description for Visually Impaired Users

This video provides an overview of Goalball, the premier Paralympic sport for athletes with visual impairments. It explains that the game is played on a court with two teams of three players each.

Key Highlights:

  • The ball contains bells, allowing players to track it by sound.
  • All players wear blackout eyeshades to ensure fair competition regardless of their level of visual impairment.
  • Players stay low to the ground, using their bodies to block the ball from entering their long, wide goal.
  • The video demonstrates the intense athleticism required to throw and block the ball at high speeds.
