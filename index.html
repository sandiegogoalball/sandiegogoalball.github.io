Welcome to our community

Empowering Athletes Through Sound and Spirit

San Diego County Goalball provides a supportive yet competitive environment for athletes of all skill levels. We are a community of athletes, coaches, and volunteers dedicated to the Paralympic sport of goalball.

Whether you're looking for recreation, fitness, or aiming for the highest levels of competition, we have a place for you on our court.
