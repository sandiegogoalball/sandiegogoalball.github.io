About Us

Building a community of strength, inclusion, and competitive excellence.

Our Story

San Diego County Goalball was born out of a family's love for the sport and a desire to create an inclusive community. Founded by Neal and Lori Meyers for their son Michael, our organization has grown from a local initiative into a premier competitive program for blind and visually impaired athletes in Southern California.

We believe that athleticism should have no boundaries. By providing accessible facilities at locations like the Stagecoach Community Center in Carlsbad and the Mission Valley YMCA, and offering expert coaching from world-class mentors like Matt Boyle, we empower our athletes to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote health, teamwork, and independence through the unique and challenging sport of goalball. We provide a supportive yet competitive environment for athletes of all skill levels, from those just starting out to those aiming for the highest levels of competition.

The Future of Goalball

With the 2028 Paralympic Games coming to Los Angeles, we are more motivated than ever to grow the sport in Southern California. We are working hard to build a pipeline of talent and a robust community of supporters to ensure that Goalball continues to thrive in our region.

Neal Meyers
