About Us

Building a community of strength, inclusion, and competitive excellence.

Our Mission

San Diego County Goalball is dedicated to providing athletic and social opportunities for individuals who are blind or visually impaired. Our mission is to promote health, teamwork, and independence through the unique and challenging sport of Goalball.

We believe that athleticism should have no boundaries. By providing accessible facilities, expert coaching, and a supportive community, we empower our athletes to reach their full potential, whether they are playing for recreation or training for elite competition.

The Future of Goalball

With the 2028 Paralympic Games coming to Los Angeles, we are more motivated than ever to grow the sport in Southern California. We are working hard to build a pipeline of talent and a robust community of supporters to ensure that Goalball continues to thrive in our region.
