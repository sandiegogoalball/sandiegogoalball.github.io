Goalball Equipment

Specialized gear designed for safety, fairness, and performance.

The Audible Ball

The official goalball is made of hard rubber and weighs 1.25kg. It features eight holes that allow the sound of the internal bells to be heard by the players.

Shop Balls

Blackout Eyeshades

Crucial for fair play, these goggles are completely opaque. They ensure that all players, regardless of their level of vision, are competing on equal terms.

Shop Eyeshades

Protective Gear

Since players dive to block balls, specialized knee, elbow, and hip padding is essential to prevent floor burns and bruising during intense play.

Shop Padding

Pro Gear Tips

Check the Bells

Always ensure your ball is in good condition and the bells are clear. A muffled ball can be a major disadvantage for your defense.

Eyeshade Comfort

Padding around the eyeshades is vital for comfort during long matches. Make sure they fit snugly but don't pinch.
