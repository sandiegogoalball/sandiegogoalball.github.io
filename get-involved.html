Get Involved

There are many ways to support San Diego Goalball. Whether you want to volunteer your time or make a donation, every bit helps.

Volunteer With Us

Our organization thrives on the dedication of our volunteers. We are always looking for help with:

  • Practice Assistance: Helping set up the court and managing equipment.
  • Refereeing & Officiating: No experience needed; we will train you!
  • Transportation: Assisting athletes with getting to and from practices.

Support Our Athletes

Donations go directly towards court rentals, equipment, and travel costs for competitions. Your support ensures that cost is never a barrier for our athletes.

How to Donate

Your contributions help us provide equipment and facilities for our athletes. All donations are greatly appreciated.
