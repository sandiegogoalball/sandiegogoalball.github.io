Sponsorship

Partner with us to empower our athletes.

Become a Partner

San Diego County Goalball is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization. We rely on the generosity of local businesses and corporate partners to sustain our programs. Sponsorship is more than just a donation—it's a partnership that demonstrates your commitment to inclusion and accessibility in sports.

Your sponsorship helps cover critical costs such as gym rentals, specialized equipment (audible balls and eyeshades), and travel expenses for regional and national competitions.

Sponsorship Levels

Bronze

$500+

  • ● Logo on website sponsors page
  • ● Social media shoutout
  • ● Mention in annual report

Silver

$1,500+

  • ● All Bronze benefits
  • ● Logo on team practice shirts
  • ● Quarterly newsletter feature

Gold

$3,000+

  • ● All Silver benefits
  • ● Logo on tournament jerseys
  • ● Prominent banner at home practices
